# B2B SaaS Onboarding FAQ

## How long does onboarding take?
Typical onboarding lasts 2 weeks: data connection (week 1) and workflow enablement (week 2).

## Do you provide sandbox accounts?
Yes. Every client receives a sandbox tenant with sample data and SSO via Okta.

## Who should attend the kickoff?
We recommend an admin, a power user, and one stakeholder to align on goals.
