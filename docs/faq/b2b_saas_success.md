# B2B SaaS Success FAQ

## How do enterprise clients submit feature requests?
Log requests under "Ideas" in the customer portal; CSMs review every Friday.

## What is the SLA for support tickets?
P1: 2 hours, P2: same business day, P3: 48 hours. The chatbot triages FAQs before escalating to Zendesk.

## Can we export usage analytics?
Yes. Navigate to Settings → Reports → Usage and click "Export CSV" for daily or weekly summaries.
