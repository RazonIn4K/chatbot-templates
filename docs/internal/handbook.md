# Internal Handbook (Sample)

## PTO & Time Off
- Submit PTO requests through BambooHR at least 7 days in advance.
- Managers approve within 2 business days; Slack #team-ops with urgent changes.

## Incident Response
1. Page the on-call engineer via PagerDuty.
2. Post a summary in #incidents.
3. Within 24 hours, add a blameless postmortem to Notion.

## System Access
- Production access granted only after completing SOC2 training.
- Use Okta for SSO; never share MFA tokens.

## Release Cadence
- Deploy to staging daily at 10 AM PT.
- Production release window: Tue/Thu 1-3 PM PT.
